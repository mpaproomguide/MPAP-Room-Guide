6th Floor Conference Room

Desktop Audio

  • Select Universal Audio Thunderbolt as your output device
  • Turn Apollo x6 volume up
  • Turn MAC pot up on the mixer
  • Turn Master Level pot up on the mixer

Laptop Audio

  • Connect to the 3.5mm jack on the table
  • Turn AUX pot up on the mixer
  • Turn Master Level pot up on the mixer

Desktop Video

  • Make sure HDMI is plugged in to the Film cart
  • Turn TV on with the remote
  • Press the home button on the remote
  • Press the left button till you see "Source"
  • Click Source and select HDMI 2

Laptop Video

  • Plug the dark grey HDMI cable into your laptop
  • Turn TV on with the remote
  • Press the home button on the remote
  • Press the left button till you see "Source"
  • Click Source and select HDMI 3
