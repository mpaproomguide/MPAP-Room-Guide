Room 770

Room PC Sound

On the podium, there is a black box labeled Crestron. Press "POWER ON" on the top left, then press the "ROOM PC" button.

On the room PC, click the Control Center icon in the top-right menu bar. Under "Sound", make sure "Crestron" is selected as the output device.

Adjust audio level with the Volume knob on the Crestron.

If you still don't have sound, make sure that neither EXT MIXER nor AUDIO MUTE have a red light beneath them on the Crestron box. If they do, press those buttons to deselect them.

When done, press "POWER OFF" on the Crestron box.

Laptop Sound

Plug the HDMI cable attached to the podium into your device.

On the podium, there is a black box labeled "Crestron". Press "POWER ON" on the top left, then press the button labeled "LAPTOP".

On the menu bar in the top right corner (if a MacBook), ensure that "Crestron" is the selected device (the circle to the left will be blue if so, click it if not).

Adjust audio level with the Volume knob on the Crestron.

Troubleshooting: If you still don't have sound, make sure that neither EXT MIXER nor AUDIO MUTE have a red light beneath them on the Crestron box. If they do, press those buttons to deselect them.

When done, press "POWER OFF" on the Crestron box.

External Sound Source

Connect your device to the aux cable.

On the podium, there is a black box labeled Crestron. Press "POWER ON" on the top left, then press the "AUX AUDIO" button.

Select External Headphones or Headphones as the audio output device on your device.

Adjust audio level with the Volume knob on the Crestron.

If you still don't have sound, make sure that neither EXT MIXER nor AUDIO MUTE have a red light beneath them on the Crestron box. If they do, press those buttons to deselect them.

When done, press "POWER OFF" on the Crestron box.
