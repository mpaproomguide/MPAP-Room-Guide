Sound Setup

Room PC Sound

RACK

  • On the top of the rack, there is a black box labeled Furman. Flip the red switch up and turn on the power.
  • On the second box in the rack labelled "Audio Control", press the button labelled "Cart".
  • Make sure the "Bass", "Treble" and "Balance" button are set to the 12 'o' clock position. Adjust the volume button as needed.
  • On the podium computer, in the menu bar in the top right corner, under sound, ensure that "SSL 2+" is the selected device (it will be highlighted blue).
  • Turn the grey knob in the top right corner of the SSL all the way to the right towards "USB".
  • Once you're done using the PC, Turn the Monitor Level knob all the way to 0 and switch off the Furman Power in the rack.

TV Monitor

  • Aim the remote labeled Samsung remote in the room at the TV and press the power button to turn it on.
  • On the remote, click the "Home" button and select HDMI 1
  • Once you are done, turn the TV off with the remote.

Laptop Sound Options

  • On the top of the rack, there is a black box labeled Furman. Flip the red switch up and turn on the power.
  • Plug the aux cable on the rack into your computer or device.
  • On the second box in the rack labelled "Audio Control", press the button labelled "Device".
  • Make sure the "Bass", "Treble" and "Balance" button are set to the 12 'o' clock position. Adjust the volume button as needed.
  • On the bottom box in the rack, make sure sure the knobs for left and right are turned to the vertical/ 12 'o' clock position
  • On your computer, in the menu bar in the top right corner, under sound, ensure that "External Headphones" is the selected device (it will be highlighted blue).
  • Once you're done using your computer, press the "Cart" button on the "Audio Control" box before unplugging the aux cord, and switch off the Furman Power in the rack.
  • When projecting video from your laptop onto the wall-mounted TV using HDMI, sound will play through the TV speakers, not the room speakers.
  • Attach the HDMI cable to your laptop and turn on the TV. If video isn't projected by default, select HDMI 2 as the input.
  • Ensure "SAMSUNG" is selected as the playback device in your computer's sound settings.

Display Settings

  1. Open System Preferences (in the dock at the bottom of the screen), click on Displays, then select the Arrangement tab.
  2. To **Mirror Displays** (same screen on cart and wall): Make sure the "Mirror Displays" box is checked.
  3. To **Extend Displays** (separate screens): Make sure the "Mirror Displays" box is unchecked. The blue box with the white tab on the top represents your computer monitor, while the other box represents the room's screen. You can move this second box to determine its relation to your monitor. For example, if placed to the left of the blue box, you can move your mouse all the way to the left of your monitor and onto the screen at the front of the room.
  1. Click the Windows icon on the bottom left corner, open 'Settings' and Click on 'System'.
  2. Click on 'Display' and scroll down to 'Multiple Displays' setting.
  3. To **Mirror Displays**: Select 'Duplicate these displays'.
  4. To **Extend Displays**: Select 'Extend these displays'.
  5. You can adjust how both screens relate to each other in the 'Rearrange your displays' section when you scroll back to the top of the page.
