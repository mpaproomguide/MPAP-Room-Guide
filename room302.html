Room 302A-D

Step 1 - Connect the Cable

  • Notice the CalDigit Thunderbolt Hub located under the computer monitor. There should be a Thunderbolt cable plugged in labeled LAPTOP or MAC MINI.
  • Unplug the Thunderbolt cable from the hub.
  • Connect the Thunderbolt cable to your laptop.

Step 2 - Choose Your Setup

  • Your laptop should now be connected to the display and audio system.
  • If you need to adjust the volume, use the volume controls on your laptop.
  • To disconnect, simply unplug the Thunderbolt cable from your laptop and plug it back into the hub.
  • The desktop computer should now be connected to the display and audio system.
  • Use the volume controls on the desktop computer to adjust the volume.
  • To switch back to laptop mode, unplug the Thunderbolt cable from the hub and connect it to your laptop.
