Room 777

Room PC Sound

  • On the podium, there is a black box labeled CRESTRON. Press "POWER ON" on the top left.
  • Press the button labeled "ROOM PC".
  • Turn the knob on the right of the Crestron box to adjust the volume.
  • Make sure that at least one of the volume bars is lit up
  • Make sure that neither EXT MIXER nor AUDIO MUTE have a red light beneath them, or press the button to deselect those options
  • On the room machine, click on the Control Center icon in the menu bar at the top right corner, under "Sound", ensure that "Crestron" is the selected device

Laptop Sound

  • On the podium, there is a black box labeled CRESTRON. Press "POWER ON" on the top left.
  • Press the button labeled "LAPTOP".
  • Plug the HDMI cable attached to the podium into your device, using one of the attached adapters if necessary.
  • Turn the knob on the right of the Crestron box to adjust the volume. Make sure that at least one of the volume bars is lit up
  • Make sure that neither EXT MIXER nor AUDIO MUTE have a red light beneath them, or press the button to deselect those options
  • On the menu bar in the top right corner (if a MacBook), ensure that "Crestron" is the selected device (the circle to the left will be blue if so, click it if not)

External Sound

  • On the podium, there is a black box labeled CRESTRON. Press "POWER ON" on the top left.
  • Press the button labeled "AUX AUDIO".
  • Connected to the podium with the audio cable to the device (with adapters if needed).
  • Turn the knob on the right of the Crestron box to adjust the volume.
  • Make sure that at least one of the volume bars is lit up
  • Make sure that neither EXT MIXER nor AUDIO MUTE have a red light beneath them, or press the button to deselect those options
    • For Mac: Click on the Control Center icon in the menu bar at the top right corner, under "Sound", ensure that "External Headphones" is the selected device (the circle to the left will be blue if so, click it if not)
    For Windows: Click on the Speaker icon in the menu bar at the lower right corner, ensure that the device start with "Headphones" is the selected device
  • When done, press "POWER OFF" on the Crestron box

Room PC Video

  • On the computer, open System Settings (in the dock at the bottom of the screen), click on displays
  • If you would like to mirror the displays (such that the screen on the podium and on the wall are the same) make sure to select Mirror for DELL P2422H. If you would like to extend the displays (such that the two screens are separate), make sure to select Extended display.
  • In the Arrange... window, you can choose the position of the monitors relative to each other, which will allow you to move your mouse between them accurately.
  • When done, press "POWER OFF" on the Crestron box

Laptop Video

MacBook:

  • Open System Settings, click on display.
  • If you would like to mirror the displays (such that the screen on the podium and on the wall are the same) make sure to select 'Mirror for Crestron'.
  • If you would like to extend the displays (such that the two screens are separate), select 'Stop Mirroring' then make sure to select Extended display. You can adjust how both screens related to each other in the arrangement tab.

Windows:

  • Click the Windows icon on the bottom left corner, open 'Settings' and Click on 'System'
  • Click on 'Display' and scroll down to 'Multiple Displays' setting.
  • If you would like to mirror the displays (such that the screen on the podium and on the wall are the same) make sure to select 'Duplicate these displays'
  • If you would like to extend the displays (such that the two screens are separate), select 'Extend these displays' You can adjust how both screens related to each other in the 'Rearrange your displays' section when scrool back to the top of the page.
