Acting Lab

Room PC Sound

Navigate to the control center located on the menu bar at the top of the screen. Select sound and make sure output is routed to SAMSUNG.

On the Yamaha sound bar, make sure the source is changed to TV. Control the volume directly from the soundbar or on the remote.

Use the volume buttons to adjust the sound level as needed.

External Audio Device

For external audio, connect device with the audio jack and ensure soundbar is switched to analog.

Room PC Video

Turn on TV with remote.

Select Source 1 labelled Acting Lab. The Mac will likely be asleep, press any key to wake up.

Laptop Video

Connect Laptop to HDMI.

Turn on TV with remote. Select source 2. Laptop will display on TV.
