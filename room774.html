Room 774

Step 1 - Connect the Cable

  • Notice the CalDigit Thunderbolt Hub located under the computer monitor. There should be a Thunderbolt cable plugged in labeled LAPTOP or MAC MINI.
  • Unplug the Thunderbolt cable from the hub.
  • Connect the Thunderbolt cable to your laptop.

Step 2 - Choose Your Setup

Laptop Mode

  • To use your personal laptop with the desk, connect the LAPTOP cable
  • To use the computer that comes with the desk, connect the MAC MINI cable

Desktop Mode

  • To use the computer that comes with the desk, connect the MAC MINI cable
  • To use your personal laptop with the desk, connect the LAPTOP cable

Tips & Tricks

  • Make sure to connect the correct cable based on your needs
  • The Thunderbolt hub provides power and display connectivity
  • If you experience any issues, try disconnecting and reconnecting the cable
