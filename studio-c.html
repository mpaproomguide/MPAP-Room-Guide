Laptop Video

Connect your laptop to the HDMI cable, located on the side of the desk

Ensure the Projector switch in the back of the room is turned on.

1/2

On the HDMI source selector on the rack, first press the PROJECTOR button to select output, then press select Laptop as input source.

1/3
Note: The order of button pressing is necessary.

On the computer, open System Settings click on displays, select MXX-A.
